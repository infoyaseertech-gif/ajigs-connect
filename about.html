AJIGS CONNECT

AJIGS CONNECT is a professionally registered cleaning and hygiene company based in Kaduna State, Nigeria. We provide comprehensive Cleaning, Laundry, Upholstery, and Fumigation services to residential and commercial clients across Kaduna.

Registered with RC Number 7304230, AJIGS CONNECT is committed to delivering world-class hygiene solutions using certified chemicals, trained personnel, and approved equipment. Every job is backed by a professional invoice.
