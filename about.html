Who We Are

AJIGS CONNECT

AJIGS CONNECT is the trading name of ARI'S JIB GLOBAL SERVICES LIMITED, a professionally registered company based in Kaduna State, Nigeria. We provide Construction, Building Materials Supply, Automobile Sales, Engineering Projects, and Cleaning Services to residential, commercial and industrial clients across Kaduna.

Registered with RC Number 7304230, AJIGS CONNECT is committed to delivering quality, reliability and value across every service we offer — backed by experienced personnel, proper materials and a professional invoice for every job.
