FAQ

Common Questions

Do you come to my location?

Yes — we come to you. Whether you are in Kawo, Barnawa, GRA, Unguwar Rimi, or anywhere in Kaduna State, our team comes to your site or address.

When do I pay?

Payment is done before work or supply begins. You can pay by Bank Transfer (Moniepoint 8069051403), Cheque, or Cash. A proper invoice is issued for every job.

How do I get a quote?

Simply call us, send a WhatsApp message, or fill the form on this page. Describe your project or service needed — we will send you a quote promptly.

Do you handle both small and large projects?

Yes. From a single room cleaning job to full construction projects, materials supply, vehicle sales, and engineering works — we scale to your needs.

How long does a job take?

It depends on the scope — a cleaning job may take a few hours, while construction or engineering projects are scheduled based on agreed timelines. We give you an estimate with your quote.
